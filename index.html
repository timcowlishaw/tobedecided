Welcome to To Be Decided, the website for BBC IRFS staff to discover how to apply agile principles (and specifically the techniques of the scrum methodology) to their work.

In particular, you'll understand how to comine agile product development with your research objectives, and how to spend more of your time at work delivering interesting, innovative products.

If you're new to Agile, this Subway map diagram from the Agile Alliance gives you a good overview of the types of techniques we'll be discussing.
